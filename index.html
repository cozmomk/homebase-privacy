Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026 — KramerOS HomeBase

About HomeBase

HomeBase is a private, household-only task coordination and family assistant application. It is not a commercial product and has no public users. All recipients of messages sent by this system are members of the Kramer household who personally configured and operate the system.

Information We Collect

HomeBase stores the following information on a private home server:

  • Task titles, descriptions, status, and activity history
  • Family notes and preferences (referred to as “memories”)
  • Inbound and outbound SMS message content
  • Google Calendar event data (read-only, via OAuth)
  • Family journal entries and photos

This data is stored in a local SQLite database on privately-owned hardware. It is not transmitted to third parties except as described below.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send daily task briefings and proactive reminders via SMS to configured household phone numbers
  • To process inbound SMS messages and respond via the AI assistant
  • To generate AI-assisted summaries and suggestions using the Anthropic Claude and Google Gemini APIs

Third-Party Services

  • Telnyx — SMS delivery and receipt. Message content is transmitted to Telnyx to send and receive text messages.
  • Anthropic — AI assistant responses are processed via the Anthropic Claude API.
  • Google — Calendar data is accessed via Google Calendar API. AI generation tasks may use the Google Gemini API.

No data is sold or shared with any other parties.

SMS Messaging

Phone numbers that receive SMS messages from HomeBase are configured manually by the household operator. Messages are sent only to household members who have opted in by being added to the system configuration.

By providing your phone number and consenting to receive SMS messages from KramerOS, you agree to receive text messages related to family task coordination, morning briefings, and household assistant notifications. Message frequency may vary.

Your mobile information will not be sold or shared with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties. We may share your Personal Data, including your SMS opt-in or consent status, with third parties that help us provide our messaging services, including but not limited to platform providers, phone companies, and any other vendors who assist us in the delivery of text messages.

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP to any message. To resume, reply START. For help, reply HELP.

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier plan. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Data Retention

All data is retained on private household hardware indefinitely unless manually deleted by the household operator. There is no automatic deletion schedule.

Contact

This is a private household system. For questions, contact the system operator directly.
